Overwatch:

What is Overwatch and how does it work?

Overwatch is a 5v5 first person shooter that has many different game modes and maps. The game modes are control point, payload, hybrid, and push. The characters have different abilities and stratagies that can interact with these maps and also can be used to make different comps such as dive or team fight.


What are the Overwatch team goals over the semester?

To make playoffs and get first in our conference.


What does the team plan on doing to improve that?

Overall communication and learning which character are currently the strongest. Also learning the new maps and modes.

Rocket League is soccer with cars that have rocket boosters allowing them to maneuver in the air.

Valorant:

What is Valorant and how does it work?

Valorant is a 5v5 first person shooter that has two teams the attackers and defenders who after 12 rounds switch sides. The attacking team is trying to make their way on site to plant a spike then defend it till it explodes. The Defender team is trying to defend the site and defuse the spike if it is planted.


What are the Valorant teams goals over the semester?

The teams goal is to make playoffs and get first in our conference.


What does the team plan on doing to improve that?

They need to work on overall communication and game skills such as aim, ability placement, and map knowledge.



League of Legends:

What is League of Legends and how does it work?

League of Legends is a MOBA which has two teams on a map with the objective being to destroy the enemies Nexus. On the way there are towers and jungle objectives that teams can take to earn more gold or buffs to help in team fights.


What are the League of Legends team goals over the semester?

This semester is a rebuilding of what we had last year. We are recruiting new players for this game and looking to build our team up for next semester.


What does the team plan on doing to improve that?

Improving communication and playing together more as a team which building team comps that make sense.
